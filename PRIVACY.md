# Privacy

Your data stays with you. Home Upkeep never asks who you are or where you live.

Your data is stored in a local database on the phone itself. That’s the only copy this app creates. There are no accounts, no sign-in, and no analytics — the app doesn’t track what you do.

## Where your data can go

- **On this device.** Everything lives in app storage on your phone.
- **In your own iCloud or Android backup, if you have it on.** Like any app, this app’s data is included in the device backup your platform makes for you. That backup is yours; nobody at Josh Approved operates it or can see it.
- **In a file you export yourself.** If you export, the app hands a single file to the system share sheet and you choose where it goes. Nothing leaves until you pick a destination.

## What’s collected

Nothing. There is no data collection, no third-party sharing, and no telemetry of any kind. Reminders are local notifications scheduled on your device; they never touch a server. Find manual opens a search in your browser only when you tap it.
